Effective Date: 2024/10/01

Ash-Blossom is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application Munni. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

When you sign up for the App, we may collect personal information, such as:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Account creation details

b. Financial Information

In order to provide you with expense tracking features, we may collect financial information that you input into the app. This includes:

  • Expenses (e.g., date, amount, category)
  • Income details (optional)

Please note: We do not access or collect any banking or credit card information directly from your financial institution.

c. Device Information

We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, such as:

  • Device ID
  • Operating system version
  • Mobile network information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Track and analyze your spending and income to provide insights and budgeting tools
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or other relevant information

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely in our system, and we follow industry best practices to protect your information.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal or financial information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With service providers who assist us in providing the App’s features and who are obligated to protect your data
  • When required by law or to respond to legal requests

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we correct, update, or delete your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we are relying on your consent to process your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at okkesdoker@outlook.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and financial data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you request to delete your account, we will delete your data as per your request, except where we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. Please review it periodically.

9. Contact Us
